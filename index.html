Market Research · Topline

Vietnam Real Estate 2026 — the data underneath.

The market signals shaping every move in Parts 1–3. Selected highlights only — full research in the linked document.

  • Q1 2026 supply. ~52,000 units offered for sale · ~38,000 new (2.5× YoY) · apartments 67% of supply · Central Vietnam 26% (rising share).
  • HCMC. VND 111M/sqm avg (+24.3% YoY) · East 85% of supply · luxury 57% of new supply · affordable segment effectively absent.
  • Hà Nội. VND 123M/sqm avg (+50% YoY) · luxury 95% of Q1 2026 supply · Tay Ho peak VND 284M/sqm.
  • Credit & rates. 8–11% home loan band · MB prime 8–8.5% · SBV directive: RE credit growth ≤ system credit growth in 2026.
  • Social housing. 158,700 units 2026 target (1.5× 2025) · accelerated to 1M units by 2028 · Hà Nội target 120,000 units 2026–2030.
  • Thủ Thiêm IFC. Inaugurated 21 Feb 2026 · 898 ha · 99-storey tower ($1.15B) · $10B initial commitments.
  • Infrastructure. Metro Line 1 corridor: +50–200% property uplift · Bến Lức – Long Thành 88% complete (Q3 2026) · Ring Road 3 phase 1 opens June 2026.
  • Northern industrial. +5,050 ha new land 2026–29 · Bắc Ninh attracted $18.6B FDI in 2025 · RBF occupancy 87%.
  • Coastal recovery. Tourism +14–27% YoY in coastal hubs · 21.1M international arrivals · Vietnam #1 for Chinese leisure travel · APEC 2027 in Phú Quốc.
  • FTSE EM upgrade. Confirmed 8 Apr 2026 · phased index inclusion from 21 Sep 2026 through 2027.
  • Marketing landscape. 71% of VN shoppers influenced by livestream · 89% follow at least one online influencer · 86% APAC distrust unverified content post-COVID.
